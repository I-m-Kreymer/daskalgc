Why Daskal

Since its founding in 2011, Daskal has built a reputation for quality work and on time delivery. From the start our focus has been on interior renovation, however in recent years we have expanded our horizons completing projects that involved bleachers, concrete, roofing, playgrounds, and much more.


We have a tremendous amount of expertise in the Public Works space. We are licensed to do Public Works and School Construction in New Jersey, New York, and licensed to do Commercial & Residential projects throughout United States.


If you are looking for an excellent work product, a reliable contractor, and an overall great partner, look no further than Daskal.
